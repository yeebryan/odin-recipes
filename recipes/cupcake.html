Cupcake

Description

Moist, perfect cupcakes everyone will love. Recipes with photos and reviews for
vanilla cupcakes, cupcake frosting, mini cupcakes and more.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour, plus more for rolling
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ½ cup cold unsalted butter, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 3 tablespoons ice water, or more as needed

Steps:

  1. For the crust, combine flour, sugar, and teaspoon salt in a food processor and pulse about 3 times until combined.
  2. Transfer dough to a work surface, and press into a single mass.
  3. Unwrap dough and place on a lightly floured work surface
  4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
